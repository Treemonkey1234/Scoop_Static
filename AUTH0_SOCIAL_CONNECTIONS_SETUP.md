## Setup Guidance for Each Provider

### 1. Google / Gmail
- **In Auth0:** Go to Authentication > Social > Google / Gmail.
- **Setup:**
  - Click "Google / Gmail".
  - Register your app at [Google Cloud Console](https://console.cloud.google.com/).
  - Get your Client ID and Client Secret.
  - Paste them into Auth0.
  - Enable the connection.

### 2. Facebook
- **In Auth0:** Go to Authentication > Social > Facebook.
- **Setup:**
  - Click "Facebook".
  - Register your app at [Facebook Developers](https://developers.facebook.com/).
  - Get your App ID and App Secret.
  - Paste them into Auth0.
  - Enable the connection.

### 3. Apple

## Next Steps

1. Register your app with each provider and collect the required credentials.
2. Add each provider in the Auth0 dashboard under Authentication > Social.
3. Paste the credentials and enable each connection.
4. Update your app's UI to show these as connect/login options.

If you want a step-by-step walkthrough for any specific provider, or have questions about the UI/UX for connecting/unlinking, just ask!
