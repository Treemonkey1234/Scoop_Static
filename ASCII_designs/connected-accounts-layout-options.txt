SPACING ANALYSIS & RECOMMENDATIONS
==================================

CURRENT ISSUES:
1. Inconsistent vertical spacing (some 16px, some 24px, some 32px)
2. Platform cards too cramped on mobile
3. Text overflow on small screens
4. Navigation controls take too much space
5. Poor visual hierarchy

RECOMMENDED SPACING SYSTEM:
- Mobile: 16px base, 24px sections, 32px major breaks
- Tablet: 20px base, 32px sections, 48px major breaks  
- Desktop: 24px base, 40px sections, 64px major breaks

GRID IMPROVEMENTS:
- Mobile: 1-2 columns max, stack platform cards vertically
- Tablet: 2-3 columns, better platform card sizing
- Desktop: 3-6 columns, full platform visibility
- XL: Consider sidebar layout for efficiency

COMPONENT SIZING:
- Platform cards: 120px min-width on mobile, 160px on desktop
- Connected account cards: Full width on mobile, max 3 per row on desktop
- Trust score section: Expand properly, better visual hierarchy
